Frequently Asked Questions (FaQ)

Quick answers to the things we hear most often.

Do you offer delivery?
Yes! We deliver within our province via GrabFood, Foodpanda, and our own riders. For areas outside the province, please contact us directly for arrangements.
Can I place a bulk or catering order?
Absolutely! We cater for birthdays, events, weddings and fiestas. Minimum of 2 dozen for bulk orders. Contact us at least 3 days in advance so we can prepare your order fresh.
Are your donuts made fresh daily?
Always! Every donut is crafted fresh every morning using locally-sourced ingredients. We don't serve reheated donuts here.
Do you have gluten-free or vegan options?
We're currently developing a gluten-free line! In the meantime, some of our fillings use plant-based ingredients. Kindly message us and we'll find something for you.
How quickly will you reply to my message?
We aim to reply within 1–2 business days via email. For urgent inquiries, give us a call or DM us on on our social media pages — we're usually faster there.
